Matching Engines are a nice example for this library because they don't just require fault tolerance they also require state management.

For example, let's say the Primary instance assembled an OrderBook of 5 orders.

If the Primary fails, the new leader must synchronize itself with the state of the last OrderBook at the time the previous leader failed.

It is not enough to simply provide a Leader Election process for detecting the failure and selecting a new leader from the Backups.
