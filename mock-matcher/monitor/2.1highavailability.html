Matching Engines are a nice example for this library because they don't just require fault tolerance they require state management. For example, let's say the Primary instance assembled an OrderBook of 5 orders. If the Primary fails, it is not enough to simply provide a Leader Election process for detecting the failure and selecting a new leader from the Backups. The new leader must synchronize itself with the state of the last OrderBook at the time the previous leader failed.
